A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

President of India from 2002 to 2007.

Biography

  • Born - 15 October 1931, Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India
  • Died - 27 July 2015, Shillong, Meghalaya, India
  • Profession - Aerospace Scientist.
  • Title - Aerospace Scientist in Defence Research and Development Organisation Indian Space Research Organisation.
  • Awards - Padma Vibhushan, Padma Bhushan, Bharat Ratna
  • Books and publications - Wings of Fire,India 2020, Ignited Minds, Target 3 Billion,
    Transcendence: My Spiritual Experiences with Pramukh Swamiji, Indomitable Spirit
